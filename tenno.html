Welcome Operator


, The Tenno are descendants of an ancient and mystical civilization of lost warriors from the Orokin era on Earth. Preserved in cryopods for centuries, the Tenno now awaken to a new war, fighting and resisting warring factions as the sole bearers of the Orokin-created Warframes. While the memories of the Tenno have faded over time, their mastery of guns, blades, and Warframe exo-armor has not. The Tenno are the driving force behind the Warframes. Harnessing void magic and wielding void amplifiers as weapons, the Tenno are a force to be reckoned with.
